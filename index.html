UPSC CSE Previous Year Questions (PYQs) — Practice Online

YESPYQ helps UPSC aspirants revise Civil Services Examination (CSE) Prelims previous year questions the smart way. Practising UPSC PYQs is one of the most reliable strategies for the Prelims — it reveals recurring themes, the examiner's pattern, and the depth of understanding the UPSC expects. Every question on YESPYQ comes with a verified answer key and a concise explanation, so each attempt doubles as a quick revision.

You can practise year-wise (2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025) to simulate a specific exam, or go subject-wise across Polity & Governance, History & Culture, Geography, Economy, Environment & Ecology, Science & Technology, and Current Affairs. It is free, needs no login, and works on mobile.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is YESPYQ?

YESPYQ is a free practice platform for UPSC CSE (Civil Services Examination) Previous Year Questions. Questions are organised subject-wise and year-wise with instant answer keys and detailed explanations.

Which years of UPSC CSE PYQs are covered?

The practice bank currently covers UPSC CSE Prelims questions from 2021 to 2025 across Polity, History, Geography, Economy, Environment, Science & Technology, and Current Affairs.

Is YESPYQ free to use?

Yes — all UPSC CSE PYQ practice on YESPYQ is completely free, with no login required.

Can I practise UPSC PYQs subject-wise and year-wise?

Yes. You can filter questions by subject (such as Polity, Geography or Economy) or by exam year, and attempt focused sets with instant feedback.

Is YESPYQ affiliated with UPSC?

No. YESPYQ is an independent study tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC).
